# Privacy Policy

**Dragon Better History** is a browser extension that helps you browse, search, and manage your own browsing history. Your privacy is fundamental to how it works.

## Data collection

Dragon Better History does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. It contains no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services.

## How your data is used

The extension reads your browsing history, recently closed tabs, and website favicons only to display them to you, locally, inside the extension's own pages. This information never leaves your browser and is never sent to any server - including ours.

Your preferences (time format, theme, number of items shown) are saved using the browser's own storage and, if you are signed in, synced across your devices by the browser itself. We have no access to this data.

## Permissions

Each permission requested by the extension is used solely to provide its features (reading and displaying history, recently closed tabs, favicons, and saving your settings). None is used to collect or share data.
